Speckle noise summary
=====================

Interpretation rule:
- Better filters reduce multiplicative speckle noise while preserving edges.
- Worse filters create blur or leave visible grain.

1.jpeg: best=bilateral_filter, worst=log_bilateral, ranking=bilateral_filter, lee_filter, tv_chambolle, gaussian_blur, median_filter, log_bilateral
5.png: best=log_bilateral, worst=bilateral_filter, ranking=log_bilateral, gaussian_blur, median_filter, tv_chambolle, lee_filter, bilateral_filter
6.jpeg: best=log_bilateral, worst=lee_filter, ranking=log_bilateral, gaussian_blur, median_filter, tv_chambolle, bilateral_filter, lee_filter

Expected pattern:
- lee_filter or log_bilateral usually works best for speckle.
- gaussian_blur is often among the worst because it removes detail together with noise.
