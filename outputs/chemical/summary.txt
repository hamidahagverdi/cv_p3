Chemical noise summary
======================

Interpretation rule:
- Better filters remove noise while preserving the lines/letters.
- Worse filters over-smooth the image or leave too much noise.

Expected pattern:
- median_filter or nl_means usually performs best for this task.
- mean_blur often performs worst because it destroys fine structures.
